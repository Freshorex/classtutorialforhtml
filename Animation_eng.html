My animation work!


Animation has always been a passion of mine, starting from animating small things on Flipaclip-

To more complex programs, like Blender (2D).

I mostly make small concept animations, with characters moving around in a void, but sometimes, I make more complex stuff, like storyboards that tell a story in particular, with dialogue and sometimes badly drawn backgrounds.

Programs I've used for 2D animation

Flipaclip

First program for animation I've touched, and while I haven't done THAT much on it, I've managed to capture its functions and how to use it effectively, layers or not.

Storyboarder

Learn it while finding an alternative for Blender at the time (now I use them simultaneously). I'm pretty efficient with it, made an entire animatic on it.


After Effects

While I would prefer not using this due to it being such a dense program, if it does the job I like using it! I don't think it's bad or good, and it was pretty easy for me to learn all things considered.

Blender

I mostly animate 2D art in this program, I've always been fascinated by the idea of mixing two or three mediums so I'm likely to learn to animate and make fully animateable models soon!
