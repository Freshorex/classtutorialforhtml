My Creative Process!


Expressing myself through art is one of my strenghts, using my emotions, my motivation and my love for the mediums I use to show parts of myself.

My art doesn't always define how I think, but my thought process making it obviously does, so I want to share some of that with you.

When I want to make an idea into reality, I think of what I want to draw, but if it doesn't come up to mind, I just draw things I'm familiar with: everyday objects, characters I've already drawn, other stuff...

Even if they aren't perfect, the way I see reality can be beautiful when other people can see it, too.

Color depends on the emotion I wanna show to the audience through my work, what I wanna convey.

My love for color will never fade, they may or not look well together, but they do the work I want them to do

Anything I MAY cherish or absolutely hate, but that doesn't mean other people won't enjoy them.

At the end of the day, I'm still an artist and I'm still human, just like you who's reading this, I may make mistakes, like:

But I learn from them.

And that's my biggest strenght, for sure.
